Vancouver Pride Parade

Hang out with friends and support diversity!
Enjoy a day of colorful scenes and colorful characters

Details

The annual Pride Parade in Vancouver is the largest parade in Western Canada and one of the largest in America.

With approximately 150 entries, including cars, floats and marching units, the only potential downside to the event is sensory overload!

Don't forget your camera, there will be famous faces to recognise like Prime Minister Justin Trudeau, Vancouver Canucks forward Emerson Etem, not to mention the larger than life drag queens and kings.

The Pride Festival kicks off at Sunset Beach around 11am and runs until 6pm near Beach Avenue and Jervois Street. There will be a Pride Market, live music, drag performers and speakers with DJs playing in the beer garden all day long.

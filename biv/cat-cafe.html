Vancouver Cat Cafe

88 West Pender Street

Vancouver British Columbia V6B 6N8

Events

Details

Vancouver Cat Cafe is a cat lover's dream!

It's a cafe serving cat-themed treats as well as a shop filled with goodies to do with anything kitty-related, all in the company of our furry feline friends!

Vancouver Cat Cafe does an amazing job in helping the BC SPCA by creating a partnership so that some of the homeless cats at the shelter have the opportunity to meet their forever families.

With that in mind, if you're having a snack or coffee at the cafe and you meet a kitty that catches your eye, please ask one of the friendly servers to get you in touch with the SPCA so you can discuss bringing your new friend home!

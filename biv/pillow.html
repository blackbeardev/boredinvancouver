International Pillow Fight

Great stress relief!

Vancouver British Columbia

Events

Details

In the global spirit of flash mobbing, be a part of this world-wide event and bring your favourite pillow down to central square for some whacking good fun!

The International Pillow Fight is held yearly and people flock to join in for some mass mob stress relief.

Please remember not to hit people too hard, feather pillows may be soft and fluffy but they can still deliver some solid pain. Also, please refrain from hitting anyone (or thing) if you don't have a pillow as this could be construed by officials (i.e. police) as assault... Keep it clean and fair people, we don't want any injuries...

The first flash mob event apparently took place in Macy's, New York, in 2003. The Oxford Dictionary defines "flash mob" as 'an unusual and pointless act' - thereby seperating it from more serious gatherings such as protests and the like.

But basically that's the point - it's not meant to be anything serious; it's all about having fun!

Grouse Mountain Zipline

6400 Nancy Greene Way
North Vancouver
604-930-9311

Details

Feeling brave? How about soaring high above the valley floor at speeds reaching 80 km/hr???

Grouse Mountains zipline is a dual-line, five-line circuit that gives you an adrenaline-pumping tour across the peaks and canyons of Grouse and Dam Mountains.

Tours are available every day from 11am and prices range from $70 - $125 depending on the season and package.

There's quite a sizeable list of rules that you'll need to be aware of, so check out their website prior to making your booking, just to make sure you're happy to go ahead - www.grousemountain.com/zipline

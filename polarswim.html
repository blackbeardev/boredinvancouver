Vancouver Polar Bear Swim

A bucket list item??
Join hundreds in the water, try not to freeze...

Details

The Vancouver Polar Bear Swim Club is one of the oldest and largest Polar Bear Clubs in the world.

The swim has grown from around 10 swimmers in it's founding year of 1920, to the new record of 2550 official entries in 2014.

Take an invigorating dip in English Bay and join thousands of hardy 'polar bears' in this free Vancouver tradition.

Feel free to dress up in costume or just come out to watch and delight in the spectacle.
